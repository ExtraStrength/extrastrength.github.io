WHAT IS WORKBENCH?

Workbench is a powerful development toolset for Framer.js. With Workbench, you can make prototypes with multiple files, with multiple people, and with a version control system, like Git. And continuously run the prototypes on your iOS device.

Workbench for Mac runs along-side your favorite text editor. It gives you boilerplate, compiles your code, and runs a local server for you.

For simple prototypes, we recommend you use the excellent Framer Studio (we use it in addition to Workbench).

Performant

Workbench precompiles Coffeescript to Javascript on your computer, so you get better performance. Your phone is faster at interpreting Javascript than Coffeescript.

Convenient

Prototypes on your phone will automatically reload as soon as you save a file on your Mac.

Installable

Add your prototypes to your homescreen for offline use and presentations.
